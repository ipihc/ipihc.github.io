International Pharmacy for Integrated Health Care

IPIHC

IPIHC provides medicine related information and helps needy people. Our platform helps people who are unable to get proper medical facilities and poor people who can't afford good medical treatments. Our vision is a world where everyone benefits from access to safe, effective, quality and affordable medicines and health technologies, as well as from pharmaceutical care services provided by pharmacists, in collaboration with other healthcare professionals. Our mission is to support global health by enabling the advancement of pharmaceutical practice, sciences and education.

Helping People

We helps people who are in need. Our platform provides them facilities without any charge.

Providing Medicines

Our platform provides free of cost medicines to needy people.

Giving Information

Apart from that, we give information about medicines and Healthcare tips.

Social Events

Our platfrom organizes events in different socities to make people aware about the importance of health, giving tips and telling them how our platfom working in this domain.
